# Support

Chess by Sparsh is a local-first browser chess app. Support is best-effort and focused on reproducible bugs, accessibility issues, documentation, and local development.

## Before Opening An Issue

- Check the [README](README.md) for setup and usage notes.
- Search existing issues and pull requests.
- Include your browser, operating system, and steps to reproduce.

## Good Issue Reports

- Describe what happened and what you expected.
- Include a FEN or move sequence when the issue involves game state.
- Mention whether the issue appears in local play, computer play, or both.

For security concerns, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
